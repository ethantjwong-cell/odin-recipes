The Best Grilled Cheese Sandwich EVER

Created by my parents 😋

Description

A delectable, quick and easy meal to make. Who doesn't like cheese inside 2 slices of bread? And more cheese on top thank you very much

Back home

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1/8th of a stick of butter
  • Approximately 200g of cheese (or more)
  • Approximately 150g of shredded cheese (optional, if not then add 150g of unshredded cheese)
  • Garlic Powder (an absolute necessity)

Cooking materials

  • Knife
  • Steel pan (preferably nonstick)
  • Cutting board
  • Stove
  • Spatula

Steps

  1. Use your knife to cut appromitaly 1/2 of your cheese, enough to cover a slice of bread(you may overlap)
  2. Sprinkle garlic powder in sandwich
  3. Now that you have your base sandwich, melt the butter in the pan
  4. Once the butter is melted, put the sandwich in the pan for about 30 seconds each side, flipping it with your spatula
  5. Put shredded cheese on the top piece of toast, then carefully flip - if you didn't shred the cheese just put slices on top
  6. Once the cheese has melted and is starting to caramelize, repeat step 4
  7. Finally, once the cheese is crispy on both sides, you may now turn off the stove and serve the grilled cheese sandwich (almost)
  8. Don't forget to sprinkle garlic powder on top (and bottom) as well!
